About Us

Redstore Athletic Choice began in 2025 as a bold dream shared by three passionate fitness enthusiasts — Redmark Sismundo, Ryuji Adriano, and Benedict Cruz. Fueled by a shared frustration with overpriced and underperforming gym apparel, they set out to create a brand that delivers style, durability, and motivation.

Our mission is to redefine athletic performance wear by creating affordable, stylish, and high-quality gear that supports every workout journey — from the gym to the street.

What started as a small idea among friends is now a growing movement of athletes, beginners, and fitness lovers committed to pushing limits and becoming stronger every day.

Our Mission

To empower individuals to reach their full potential through functional, stylish, and durable fitness apparel and gear.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and inspiring athletic brand for every body and every goal — globally recognized for quality and community.

Core Values

  • Strength: We build gear that lasts and inspires strength in all forms.
  • Passion: Born from a love for fitness, passion drives everything we do.
  • Community: We lift each other up — in the gym and in life.
  • Affordability: Quality shouldn't be a luxury. We make excellence accessible.
